IKO-Bin

Creating an entire new world of waste free streets

IKO-Bin is an intelligent waste solution for public areas. It simplifies recycling and compacting waste. The bin also tracks its fill level and gathers data for efficient waste management.

Why IKO-Bin?

IKO-Bin is a smart waste solution designed to simplify recycling and compact waste in public areas. It promotes sustainability, optimizes waste collection, and supports a cleaner, healthier environment.


  • Optimized Resources: Efficient use of resources, reducing operational costs.

  • Eco-Friendly: Encourages sustainable practices and minimizes waste impact.

  • Community Health: Prevents waste overflow and supports community well-being.

  • Smart Tech: Uses advanced technology for real-time monitoring and efficient waste collection.

Why is IKO-Bin important?

Improper waste disposal and the resulting accumulation have far-reaching consequences on the environment and public health. It contributes to land, water, and air pollution, which in turn accelerates climate change and disrupts ecosystems. Contaminants from waste can seep into soil and waterways, poisoning plants, animals, and humans. The toxic gases released during decomposition degrade air quality, posing respiratory risks and exacerbating global warming. Additionally, waste buildup leads to habitat destruction, threatening biodiversity and causing species loss. These issues not only harm the environment but also increase the prevalence of diseases, endanger food security, and undermine the quality of life for communities worldwide.

Our Solution

How IKO-Bin works

IKO-Bin: Where Trash Meets it's Place

The IKO-Bin is an advanced street dumpster designed to revolutionize waste management with cutting-edge features. Equipped with smart auto compression, solar-powered energy, lithium batteries, and trash capacity detection systems, it ensures efficient waste collection and monitoring. Designed for local governments (LGUs) and citizens by streamlining maintenance and improving garbage collection processes. By reducing waste buildup on roads and pathways, IKO-Bin promotes cleaner surroundings and enhances efficiency for waste collectors, contributing to a more sustainable and organized waste management system.

Our app

The app

IKO-Mobile: Find all IKO-Bin's near you

Our app is an all-in-one tool that provides all the data you need, including waste collection statistics, real-time bin fill levels, and technical status updates. It’s the perfect platform for optimizing waste management and taking your waste management practices to a whole new level.

Advanced waste data analytics for optimized waste management

  • A wide range of waste collection statistics, including exportable reports

  • Ability to determine the operating time of the device, using the scheduling feature

  • Real-time waste capacity monitoring of each IKO-Bin's in an area

  • Recieve personalized notifications of recently collected dumpsters

  • Personalize and customize to your own preferences
