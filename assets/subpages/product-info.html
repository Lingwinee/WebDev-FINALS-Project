How IKO-Bin works

IKO-Bin: Where Trash Meets it's Place

Our flagship product features cutting-edge technology that enhances recycling interactivity and elevates waste management efficiency to new heights.
An product contains numerous innovative features simplifying and enhancing the effeciency of waste collection and management.


Product Features

Solar Powered Dumpster

IKO-Bin does not require direct power supply it relies on solar, promoting enerygy conservation and greener environment.

Four Compartments Segregation

General, Recyclable, Compostable, and Hazardous are all there to promote and enhance waste management.

Compression General & Recyclable

General and Recyclable's get compressed to decrease the volume and lower the frequency of bin emptying.

Fill level control & alerts

Fill level sensors check whether the bin needs to be emptied. Our collectors will be notified the bins are full.

Real-time location monitoring

Our IKO-Bin contains GPS to locate and accurately calculate fastest route for effecient waste collection.

App for stats & reports

Our app provides you available dumpsters near you, data for optimizing waste collection and management.
